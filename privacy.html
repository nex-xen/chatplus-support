Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24-12-2025

ChatPlus provides an AI-powered productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed and protected when you use the ChatPlus mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

No Account Required

You can use ChatPlus without creating an account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any government-issued identifiers.

Information You Choose to Provide

To deliver app features, ChatPlus processes content that you submit, including: text input, files and images uploaded for analysis, and voice input used to convert speech into text.

This information is used only to perform the actions you request and is not used for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

AI Processing and Third-Party Services

To generate responses, ChatPlus securely transmits user-submitted content to trusted third-party AI service providers via encrypted connections.

ChatPlus does not use your content to train AI models. Third-party providers process content only to generate responses and provide requested functionality.

While ChatPlus does not store your content on its own servers, third-party providers may retain submitted data for a short, limited period to ensure service safety and prevent abuse.

Data Storage and Retention

Chat history is stored locally on your device. We do not host conversations on our servers. Uploaded files and voice data are processed temporarily and deleted shortly after processing, typically within 60 seconds.

Technical Diagnostics

We collect limited technical diagnostics such as device type, operating system version, and crash information. This data is not linked to your identity and does not include user-submitted content.

Notifications

ChatPlus may send notifications related to app functionality, service updates, or subscription status. Notifications are sent only with your permission and are never promotional.

Payments and Subscriptions

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. ChatPlus does not collect or store payment information.

User Control and Deletion

You may delete chats or clear conversation history within the App. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device.
