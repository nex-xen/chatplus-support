ChatPlus Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24-12-2025

ChatPlus provides an AI-powered productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed and protected when you use the ChatPlus mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

No Account Required

You can use ChatPlus without creating an account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any government-issued identifiers.

Information You Choose to Provide

To deliver app features, ChatPlus processes content that you submit, including:

  • Text input: Messages and prompts you send to the AI.
  • Files and images: Documents or images you upload for analysis.
  • Voice input: Audio used to convert speech into text.

This information is used only to perform the actions you request. It is not used for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

AI Processing and Third-Party Services

To generate responses, ChatPlus securely transmits user-submitted content to trusted third-party AI service providers via encrypted connections.

  • ChatPlus does not use your content to train AI models.
  • Third-party service providers process content only to generate responses and provide requested functionality.
  • While ChatPlus does not store your content on its own servers, third-party providers may retain submitted data for a short, limited period solely to ensure service safety and identify platform abuse, in accordance with their security requirements.

Data Storage and Retention

We follow a privacy-first, minimal-retention approach.

  • Chat history is stored locally on your device. We do not host your conversations on our servers.
  • Uploaded images, files, and voice data are processed in temporary memory and are automatically deleted shortly after processing, typically within 60 seconds.
  • If you delete chats or remove the App, the data is removed from your device and cannot be recovered by ChatPlus.

Technical Diagnostics

To keep the App stable, we collect limited technical diagnostics data, such as device type, operating system version, and crash information. This data is not linked to your identity or your submitted content. We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

Notifications

ChatPlus may send notifications related to app functionality, service updates, or subscription status. Notifications are sent only if you grant permission through the iOS system-level consent prompt. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device settings. We do not send advertising or promotional notifications.

Payments and Subscriptions

All purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. ChatPlus does not collect or store payment details. We receive only an anonymized confirmation to verify subscription status. Subscription management and cancellations are handled through your Apple ID settings.

User Control and Deletion

You remain in control of your data. You can delete individual chats or clear all conversation history within the App settings. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device.
