ChatPlus Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24-12-2025

ChatPlus provides an AI-powered productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed and protected when you use the ChatPlus mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

No Account Required

You can use ChatPlus without creating an account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any government-issued identifiers.

Information You Choose to Provide

To deliver app features, ChatPlus processes content that you submit, including:

  • Text input: Messages and prompts you send to the AI.
  • Files and images: Documents or images you upload for analysis.
  • Voice input: Audio used to convert speech into text.

This information is used only to perform the actions you request. It is not used for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

AI Processing and Third-Party Services

To generate responses, ChatPlus securely transmits user-submitted content to trusted third-party AI service providers via encrypted connections.

  • ChatPlus does not use your content to train AI models.
  • Third-party service providers process content only to generate responses and provide requested functionality.
  • While ChatPlus does not store your content on its own servers, third-party providers may retain submitted data for a short, limited period solely to ensure service safety and identify platform abuse.

Data Storage and Retention

We follow a privacy-first, minimal-retention approach.

  • Chat history is stored locally on your device.
  • Uploaded data is automatically deleted shortly after processing, typically within 60 seconds.
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

Technical Diagnostics

Limited diagnostics such as device type and OS version are collected to keep the App stable.
