Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24-12-2025

ChatPlus provides an AI-powered productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed and protected when you use the ChatPlus mobile application.

No Account Required

You can use ChatPlus without creating an account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any government-issued identifiers.

Information You Choose to Provide

ChatPlus processes content you submit, including text prompts, uploaded files or images, and voice input used for speech-to-text conversion.

AI Processing & Third-Party Services

User content is securely transmitted to trusted third-party AI providers to generate responses. ChatPlus does not use your content to train AI models.

Data Storage & Retention

Chat history remains on your device. Uploaded data is processed temporarily and deleted shortly after processing, typically within 60 seconds.

Notifications

Notifications are optional, system-controlled, and never promotional.
