About Chat Plus

Chat Plus is a modern AI-powered assistant built to simplify everyday tasks, enhance creativity, and help users interact with information in smarter ways. Our goal is to bring powerful AI capabilities into one clean, easy-to-use platform that works seamlessly across different needs.

With Chat Plus, users can chat with AI for answers, ideas, and guidance, generate and refine written content, and create AI-generated images. The app also supports voice input, allowing users to interact naturally using speech. Through OCR (optical character recognition), Chat Plus can extract text from images you choose to scan or upload, making it easy to convert visuals into editable and usable text.

Chat Plus also allows users to upload images, documents, and files for analysis, summaries, and content generation, helping turn raw information into clear insights. All features are designed to be user-initiated and focused on productivity, learning, and creativity.

Privacy is a core part of Chat Plus. User content is processed only to provide requested features and is not permanently stored. We are committed to providing a secure and transparent experience while delivering intelligent tools that adapt to how people work and create.

Chat Plus is built for students, professionals, and creators who want a reliable AI companion that is powerful, flexible, and easy to use.
